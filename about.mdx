Welcome to **Lotro Delver**, your new WebApp for Lord of the Rings Online! The Lotro Delver is a new WebApp designed to provide information about the game, currently focusing primarily on in-game items. The app is in active development (Alpha stage), with many exciting features planned for the future. The goal is to continually improve the Lotro Delver by adding new features based on your feedback and suggestions. It is completely free to use and ad-free, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience for our users.

Be sure to check out our roadmap to see what features are in development and join our Discord server to connect with other players and stay updated on the latest news and developments.

Thank you and happy gaming!
